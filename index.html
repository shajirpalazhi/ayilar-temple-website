About the Temple

"Nestled in peaceful surroundings, Ayilara Madathil Sree Bhadra Bhagavathi Temple is a sacred center dedicated to Sree Bhadra Bhagavathi, the divine Mother of strength, protection, and prosperity. The temple’s serene ambience, traditional Kerala architecture, and timeless rituals offer devotees spiritual solace and blessings. Annual festivals and poojas bring the community together in devotion, making it a place of faith, peace, and divine grace."
